How to find your Session Key:

  1. Open claude.ai in Chrome
  2. Right click anywhere and select "Inspect" (or press F12)
  3. Go to Application tab (might need to click >> to find it)
  4. On the left, expand "Cookies" and click on "https://claude.ai"
  5. Find sessionKey in the list
  6. Copy the "Value" (starts with sk-ant-sid)
